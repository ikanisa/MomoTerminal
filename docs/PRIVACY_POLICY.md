# Privacy Policy for MomoTerminal

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## Introduction

MomoTerminal ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Merchant Information**: Phone number, merchant code, and business name for transaction processing
- **Transaction Data**: Payment amounts, transaction IDs, timestamps, and payment status
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers

### Automatically Collected Information
- **SMS Data**: We read incoming SMS messages to detect and process mobile money payment confirmations. We only process messages from known mobile money providers (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo).
- **NFC Data**: When using NFC tap-to-pay, we collect payment amount and merchant code for transmission to the payer's device.
- **Usage Analytics**: App usage patterns, feature usage, and performance metrics to improve the application.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Process and record mobile money transactions
- Sync transaction data with your business systems
- Provide NFC tap-to-pay functionality
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Send transaction notifications and confirmations
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Transaction data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database
- Sensitive data (API keys, merchant codes) is stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences

### Cloud Sync
- Transaction data may be synced to our secure servers for backup and business reporting
- All data transmission uses HTTPS with certificate pinning
- We use Firebase services (Crashlytics, Analytics, Performance) for app monitoring

### Security Measures
- Data encryption at rest and in transit
- Secure authentication mechanisms
- Regular security audits and updates
- Biometric authentication support

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase**: Crash reporting, analytics, and performance monitoring
- **Google Play Services**: SMS retrieval and authentication

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## Data Retention

- Transaction data is retained on the device until manually deleted
- Server-side data is retained as per your business requirements
- Crash logs and analytics data are retained for 90 days

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your transaction data
- Opt-out of analytics collection

## Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for use by children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
