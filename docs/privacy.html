Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 1, 2025

Introduction

MomoTerminal ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for Mobile Money POS terminal services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Merchant Information: Phone number, merchant code, and business name for transaction processing and account management
  • Transaction Data: Payment amounts, transaction IDs, timestamps, payment status, and currency information
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app optimization and security

Automatically Collected Information

  • SMS Data: We read incoming SMS messages to detect and automatically process mobile money payment confirmations. We only process messages from known mobile money providers (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo in Ghana; MTN, Airtel, Tigo, Vodacom, M-Pesa in East Africa). We do not access or store your personal SMS messages.
  • NFC Data: When using NFC tap-to-pay functionality, we collect payment amount and merchant code for transmission to the payer's device. This data is processed locally and not permanently stored.
  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature usage, screen views, and performance metrics to improve the application and identify bugs.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process and record mobile money transactions
  • Sync transaction data with your business systems via custom webhooks
  • Provide NFC tap-to-pay terminal functionality
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send transaction notifications and confirmations
  • Provide customer support and troubleshooting
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Transaction data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database using SQLCipher with AES-256 encryption
  • Sensitive data (API keys, merchant codes, authentication tokens) is stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences backed by the Android Keystore
  • Database backups are disabled to prevent unencrypted data extraction

Cloud Sync (Optional)

  • Transaction data may be synced to our secure Supabase servers for backup and business reporting (if enabled)
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS with TLS 1.2+ and certificate pinning
  • Server-side data is encrypted at rest

Security Measures

  • End-to-end data encryption (at rest and in transit)
  • Secure authentication with biometric support (fingerprint, face unlock)
  • Device security verification (root detection)
  • Screen security to prevent screenshots of sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Webhook requests signed with HMAC for authenticity

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide and improve our application:

  • Supabase: Backend authentication, database sync, and edge functions. Data is stored in secure PostgreSQL databases. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google): Crash reporting (Crashlytics), analytics, and performance monitoring. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services: SMS retrieval API, location services (for fraud prevention), and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI: SMS parsing with on-device fallback. Messages are processed securely and not stored by Google. Terms
  • Custom Webhooks: Transaction data may be sent to merchant-configured webhook URLs for business system integration. Merchants are responsible for securing their webhook endpoints.

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your data (e.g., custom webhooks)
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our application (listed above)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified)
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues

Data Retention

  • Transaction Data: Retained on your device until manually deleted. Server-side backups retained for 2 years or as per your business requirements
  • Crash Logs & Analytics: Retained for 90 days for debugging and improvement purposes
  • Account Data: Retained until you request account deletion
  • Archived Data: Financial records may be retained longer to comply with tax and accounting regulations

Your Rights (GDPR & Data Protection)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your transaction data in CSV or JSON format
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of analytics and marketing communications
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions (SMS, NFC) at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@momoterminal.com.

App Permissions Explained

MomoTerminal requires the following permissions:

  • SMS (READ_SMS, RECEIVE_SMS): To automatically detect and log mobile money payment confirmations. We only read messages from mobile money providers, not your personal SMS.
  • NFC: To provide tap-to-pay terminal functionality for customer payments
  • Phone (CALL_PHONE): To dial USSD codes for manual payment initiation
  • Biometric: For secure authentication using fingerprint or face unlock
  • Camera: For barcode scanning (optional feature)
  • Internet: For syncing data and accessing cloud features
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback during transactions

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, though some features may not work without them.
