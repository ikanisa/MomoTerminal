MomoTerminal - Mobile Money POS

A comprehensive Mobile Money Point-of-Sale (POS) system for merchants. Accept payments via NFC tap-to-pay and track transactions in real-time.

Key Features:
• NFC Terminal Mode - Turn your phone into a payment terminal
• SMS Relay - Automatic transaction tracking and sync
• Multi-Provider Support - MTN, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo
• Offline Support - Works without internet, syncs when online
• Secure - End-to-end encryption for all transactions
